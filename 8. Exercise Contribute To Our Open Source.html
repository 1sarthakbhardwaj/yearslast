If you haven't done so already, it is time to contribute to your first open source project! The contribution can be as big as a new feature, to as small us fixing a typo in the README.md file. Here is what we are going to do: (Heads up! It's now a 4 step process instead of a 5 step process)


1. Join our Discord community and make sure you are in the #group-project channel. 

2. Read the git-started-guide which is attached to this lecture as extra resource. It is a PDF file that will show you exactly what to do thanks to one of our fellow students @Wan (Make sure you message her on Discord to thank her for this beautiful masterpiece)

3. Contribute ONE Pull Request to open source projects in our new community: https://github.com/zero-to-mastery

4. Share your pull request on the Discord channel #group-project so others can see your awesome contribution!

5. In order to see the Zero to Mastery Icon in your Github profile, follow this: https://help.github.com/articles/publicizing-or-hiding-organization-membership/
